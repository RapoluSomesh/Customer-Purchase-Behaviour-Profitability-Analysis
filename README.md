# 📊 Customer Purchase Behaviour & Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing patterns, revenue trends, and product profitability to identify key business drivers and improvement opportunities.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib (Data Visualization)
- SQL (Data Extraction & Aggregation)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains structured transactional business data including:

- Order ID  
- Customer ID  
- Order Date  
- Region  
- Product Category  
- Quantity  
- Sales  
- Cost  

### 🧮 Engineered Features

- Profit = Sales − Cost  
- Profit Margin (%)  
- Month (Extracted from Order Date)

---

## 🔎 Key Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend
- Identified peak and low-performing months.
- Analyzed seasonal revenue fluctuations.

### 2️⃣ Customer Retention Analysis
- Measured repeat purchase rate.
- Calculated customer retention percentage.

### 3️⃣ Regional Performance
- Evaluated region-wise revenue distribution.
- Identified underperforming markets.

### 4️⃣ Product Category Contribution
- Determined top revenue-generating categories.
- Analyzed category-wise profit contribution.

### 5️⃣ Profitability Analysis
- Calculated profit and profit margin.
- Evaluated margin distribution across transactions.

### 6️⃣ Key Business Metrics
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Customer Retention Rate
- Overall Profit Margin

## 📊 Key Insights

- 📅 May recorded the highest revenue.
- 🌍 North region generated maximum revenue.
- 🏆 Office Supplies product category drives majority of business revenue.
- 🔁 Customer Retention Rate: 90.7%.
- 💰 Average Order Value (AOV): ₹2.6K.
- 📊 Overall Average Profit Margin: 37%.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts during high-performing months.
- Improve operational efficiency in underperforming regions.
- Promote high-margin product categories.
- Introduce customer loyalty programs to increase retention rate.
- Optimize pricing strategies for low-margin products.
